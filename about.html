Who We Are

Visit Wolayta Tourism Agency, based in Wolaita Sodo, Ethiopia, is a premier tour operator dedicated to showcasing the hidden treasures of the Wolayta region. Established in 2015, our agency is licensed by the Ethiopian Ministry of Culture and Tourism and is committed to promoting sustainable tourism that preserves Wolayta’s rich heritage while uplifting local communities. We specialize in crafting personalized travel experiences that highlight the region’s unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty.

Wolayta, located 300 km south of Addis Ababa, is a land of royal heritage with over 50 kings across four dynasties, vibrant festivals like Gifaataa, and breathtaking attractions such as Ajora Falls and Mount Damota. Our passionate team of local experts ensures every journey is immersive, authentic, and unforgettable.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional travel experiences that celebrate Wolayta’s cultural heritage, natural wonders, and community spirit, while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local residents and preserve the environment for future generations.

Our Vision

To establish Wolayta as a must-visit destination on the global tourism map, recognized for its rich history, vibrant culture, and unparalleled natural beauty, fostering economic growth and cultural pride in the region.

Our Services

Cultural & Historical Tours

Explore Wolayta’s royal past with guided tours to historical sites like the Mochena Borago Rockshelter and the legacy of Kawo Tona Gaga, the last king of the Wolaita Kingdom.

Nature & Adventure Trips

Discover the stunning landscapes of Ajora Falls, Mount Damota, and the lush forests along the Ajacho River, with hiking and eco-tourism adventures tailored to all levels.

Community-Based Experiences

Engage with Wolayta’s vibrant culture through village visits, coffee cycle tours, and participation in festivals like Gifaataa, fostering meaningful connections with local communities.
